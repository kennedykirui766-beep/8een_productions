About Us

Where street stories meet cinematic excellence.

Our Story

8een Productions is a Nairobi-born film and creative studio dedicated to telling youth-driven, emotionally rich, and visually powerful stories for the modern world. Rooted in the streets, shaped by real conversations, and polished for global screens, we create films, music visuals, and digital content that reflect authentic African experiences with cinematic quality.

We believe every story deserves more than just a camera — it deserves intention, emotion, and craft. From raw dialogue scenes on a city bench to high-energy music videos and branded content, our work blends street realism with professional storytelling.

At 8een Productions, we don't chase trends — we build moments that last. Our commitment to authenticity and emotional depth sets us apart in an industry often dominated by spectacle over substance.

Vision

To become a leading African creative studio known for producing emotionally powerful, youth-centered stories that connect local culture to a global audience. We envision a future where African narratives are told with authenticity, cinematic excellence, and universal appeal.

Mission

To create meaningful visual content that elevates African voices, empowers young talent, and delivers high-quality storytelling for artists, brands, and digital platforms. We are committed to nurturing the next generation of visual storytellers while maintaining the highest standards of craft.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we create.

Authenticity

Authenticity over imitation. We value real stories told in real ways.

Emotion

Emotion over excess. Every frame should make you feel something genuine.

Story First

Story before spectacle. Narrative is the foundation of everything we do.

Collaboration

Growth through collaboration. We rise by lifting others in the creative community.

Meet the Founder

Marvin Marisin Kipruto

Founder & Creative Director

Marvin Marisin Kipruto is a Nairobi-based filmmaker, editor, and creative director with a passion for youth storytelling and urban cinema. With a background in film production and digital media, Marvin founded 8een Productions to create a platform where authentic street stories meet professional filmmaking standards.

His work focuses on emotional dialogue, youth culture, music visuals, and cinematic storytelling that reflects modern African identity. Through 8een Productions, he continues to develop short films, web series, and creative collaborations aimed at building a new generation of African visual storytelling.
