Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive creative services including film production, music video direction, video editing, photography, scriptwriting, and social media content production.

How do I start a project?

Simply fill out the contact form above or email us directly. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your vision, requirements, and timeline.

What is your typical turnaround time?

Turnaround varies by project scope. Music videos typically take 2-4 weeks, while film projects may require several months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during consultation.

Do you work with clients outside Kenya?

Absolutely! We work with clients globally and are experienced in remote collaboration. We can also travel for international shoots when needed.
